About Me

Hi, my name is Mukul, and I work as Associate Machine Learning Scientist at Wadhwani AI where I work on building AI solutions for healthcare problems for the underserved communities in India. These days I am intrigued by LLMs and have been working on creating health assistant chatbots for healthcare workers.

I recently published and presented a paper in AAAI Conference 2024 on Automating Line Probe Assay Tests in Tuberculosis .

I studied at IIIT-Delhi where I pursued an interdisciplinary course in the intersection of Computer Science, Economics and Sociology.

I have interned with Wadhwani AI in the past helping build AI and data science solutions to predict non-adherence in TB patients(Read about my experience here). I have also worked with TCPD in 2019, helping them with creating datasets for legislators and various election related judgements. In my sophomore year, I worked with Prof. Ralph Shroeder (OII, University of Oxford) and Dr. Anupam Das (IIM Kozhikode) to build a twitter archive of Narendra Modi and Rahul Gandhi for analysis during 2019 General Elections (LOR by Ralph).

You can find me on GitHub and Linkedin . Feel free to take a look at my resume or contact me here.
